About

VALORANT

valorant is a free-to-play first-person hero shooter developed and published by Riot Games, for Microsoft Windows. The game was officially released on June 2, 2020, after a closed beta period that began in April 2020. The game features a roster of agents, each with unique abilities, and players compete in teams to complete objectives and eliminate the opposing team.
